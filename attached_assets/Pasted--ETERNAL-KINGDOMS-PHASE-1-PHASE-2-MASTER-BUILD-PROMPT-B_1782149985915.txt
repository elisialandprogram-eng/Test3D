LAND SYSTEM
====================================================================

Internal Only.

Never visible.

World contains:

65536 lands

Layout:

256 x 256

Land Size:

12 x 12 coordinates

Formula:

LandX = floor(X / 12)

LandY = floor(Y / 12)

LandID =
(LandY * 256)
+ LandX
+ 1

Store internally.

Never render.

Never display.

Never draw boundaries.
